How it works

Number the fingers of each hand 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 starting at the thumb. To multiply two numbers from 6 to 10:

  • Fold down every finger up to and including your number on each hand — those are the orange fingers.
  • Tens = add all the folded orange fingers together, then ×10.  (e.g. 2 + 2 = 4 → 40)
  • Ones = multiply the raised blue fingers: (left up) × (right up).  (e.g. 3 × 3 = 9)
  • Answer = tens + ones.  7 × 7 = 40 + 9 = 49.
